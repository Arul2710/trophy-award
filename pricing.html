Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and services.

What is the typical turnaround time for an order?
Standard production takes 3 business days for Basic plans, 2 days for Standard, and 1 day for Premium. Enterprise bulk orders are typically completed within 5–7 business days, though rush delivery (24 hours) is available on select plans. Turnaround begins after artwork approval.
Can I request a completely custom trophy design?
Absolutely. Our Premium Crystal and Enterprise Custom plans include full custom design services. You can submit sketches, photos, or describe your vision, and our in-house design team will create a digital proof for your approval. Basic and Standard plans offer pre-designed templates that can be personalized with text and logos.
What materials are available for trophies and awards?
We offer a wide range of materials including gold/silver/brass metal figures, crystal and glass bases, natural wood and marble bases, acrylic and resin options, and eco-friendly bamboo materials. Premium and Enterprise plans have access to all materials, while Basic and Standard plans include our standard material selections.
Do you offer discounts for bulk or corporate orders?
Yes! Our Enterprise Custom plan is designed specifically for bulk orders of 10 or more units at a flat rate of $499. For larger quantities (50+ units), we offer tiered volume discounts. We also provide annual pricing agreements for corporate clients who place recurring orders throughout the year. Contact us for a custom quote.
How does shipping work and what are the costs?
We ship nationwide via trusted carriers. Standard shipping is $9.99 for most orders and takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping (1–2 days) is available for $19.99. The Enterprise Custom plan includes free nationwide shipping with tracking and insurance. International shipping is available upon request with additional charges.
What is your revision and refund policy?
We want you to love your award. Each plan includes a set number of free revisions: 1 for Standard, 2 for Premium, and 3 for Enterprise. Basic plans include one proof review before production. Once production begins, cancellations are subject to a 50% fee. If your order arrives damaged, we will replace it free of charge within 14 days.
