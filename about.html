Our Story

TrophyCraft was born in 2005 in a modest 400-square-foot workshop on the outskirts of Austin, Texas. Founder Daniel Russo, a master engraver with fifteen years of experience in the awards industry, saw a need for truly personalized, handcrafted recognition pieces that told a story beyond the standard catalog offerings.

By 2010, we moved into a 3,000-square-foot facility and added laser engraving technology. In 2016, we expanded again, investing in CNC routing and rotatory engraving capabilities that allowed us to work with acrylic, glass, marble, and exotic hardwoods. Today, our 14-person team serves clients nationwide from our 8,000-square-foot studio, but we still approach every order with the same workshop mentality: each piece is a reflection of someone's achievement, and it deserves nothing less than our best.
