Service 02

Advanced Laser Engraving

Our state-of-the-art CO2 and fiber laser engraving systems represent the pinnacle of marking technology. With a beam diameter as fine as 0.001 inches, we can etch intricate designs, photographic images, and even 3D textures onto almost any surface. Unlike traditional rotary engraving, laser processing is contact-free, eliminating mechanical stress on delicate materials.

  • Crystal & Glass Etching — 3D subsurface laser engraving creates floating images inside optical crystal. Ideal for elegant corporate awards and treasured keepsakes.
  • Metal Marking & Annealing — Fiber laser annealing produces high-contrast black or colored marks on stainless steel, titanium, brass, and aluminum without abrasion.
  • Wood & Acrylic Cutting — Our CO2 laser cuts and engraves plywood, MDF, acrylic sheet, leather, and fabric with clean, polished edges that need no secondary finishing.
  • Full-Color & Photo Engraving — Grayscale laser marking on anodized aluminum and coated metals produces photographic-quality portraits and detailed artwork.

Our Work Process

A streamlined four-step approach that takes your award from initial concept to final delivery — with transparency and craftsmanship at every stage.

01

Consultation

We begin with a free, no-obligation conversation about your project. Discuss your vision, budget, timeline, and preferred materials. Our experts ask the right questions to ensure we capture every detail before a single line is drafted.

02

Design

Our creative team produces detailed 2D vector layouts and photorealistic 3D renderings of your award. We share proofs digitally for your review, make revisions at no extra charge, and lock the design only when you're 100% satisfied.

03

Production

Skilled craftsmen and precision machinery bring the design to life. We select, cut, and prepare materials, perform the engraving or etching, then hand-finish, polish, and assemble every component with meticulous quality control at each step.

04

Delivery

Each award is carefully wrapped, padded, and boxed in custom protective packaging. We ship via insured carriers with real-time tracking and signature confirmation. For local clients, complimentary workshop pickup is also available.
