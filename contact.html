Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about ordering, shipping, and working with TrophyCraft.

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping within the continental US takes 3–5 business days via FedEx Ground or UPS. Express shipping (1–2 business days) is available for an additional $12.99. Orders shipped to Alaska, Hawaii, and US territories typically arrive within 5–8 business days. International shipping timelines vary by destination — we'll provide a tracking number and estimated delivery date when your order ships.
Can I rush my order?
Yes! We offer rush production services for an additional fee. Our fastest turnaround is 24 hours from artwork approval for select products. Standard rush (48 hours) is available on most trophy and plaque orders, and super rush (same-day) can be accommodated for small engraving jobs. Rush orders placed before 12:00 PM EST can often ship the same day. Call us to confirm availability for your specific project.
Do you offer international shipping?
Absolutely. We ship to over 40 countries worldwide including Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, Germany, Japan, and the United Arab Emirates. International shipping rates are calculated by weight, dimensions, and destination. All international orders include full-value insurance and customs documentation. Please note that the recipient is responsible for any import duties, taxes, or customs clearance fees that may apply.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and PayPal for online and phone orders. We also accept wire transfers and ACH payments for corporate and bulk orders over $500. For local customers, we accept cash and certified checks for in-person pickup. Net-30 terms are available for qualified educational institutions and government agencies — please contact our billing department to set up an account.
Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?
Yes, we offer sample production for bulk and corporate orders of 10+ units. A sample unit is produced at 50% of the unit price and includes full engraving and finishing. Once you approve the sample, the cost of the sample is deducted from your final bulk order. Samples typically ship within 3–5 business days from artwork approval. We also maintain a physical showroom where you can view and handle our standard material and finish options.
Do you offer design proofs?
Every order includes a free digital design proof before production begins. Our in-house design team creates a detailed 2D vector layout or photorealistic 3D rendering of your award, including your engraving text, logo placement, and color selections. You may request up to two rounds of revisions at no charge. Additional revision rounds beyond two are billed at a flat $25 fee. Proofs are typically delivered within 24–48 hours after order placement.
